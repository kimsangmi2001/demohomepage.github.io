The future of the 4th industrial revolution in 10 years


Written by the Institute for Future Strategy and Policy

What should we try to prepare for the Fourth Industrial Revolution?





Continuous changes are important



I read this book cause i wanted to know what type
of industries and fields would be bright when i
decide my career. I haven't yet decided what i
want to do for a living. So, i thought that this
book might show me the type of jobs that would be
very noticed in the future, and i was kind of
right.

We all know that the technology is developing
rapidly. And this means that to keep up with the
technology, we should learn the updating
informations fast, and predict the next upcomming
star fields.

The book says that 49% of all jobs will be at
risk from automation. Those who cannot work smart
will be deprived of their jobs by artificial
intelligence. Nevertheless, the Fourth Industrial
Revolution will give us new opportunities. For
example, a 3D printer threatens who are working
as a jewelry and shoes Industrial designer,
architecture, construction worker, aerospace
workers, dental and medical workers, etc. But
there would be new jobs like 3D printer material
expert, 3D printer ink developer, 3D printer
fashion designer, 3D food printer cook.

Google founder and CEO Larry Page said ,
"The reason for the company's failure is that it did not predict the future".
